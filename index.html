About Best Aesthetics

Hello, I’m Iwona, founder of Best Aesthetics — a nurse-led aesthetics clinic based in Appleford, welcoming clients from Didcot, Abingdon, Oxford and the surrounding Oxfordshire areas.

I began my journey in 2010, when my clinic was originally known as Best Beauty on Wilkins Road. During those early years, I built a loyal client base, meeting thousands of wonderful clients — many of whom continue to trust me with their treatments today.

In 2013, my passion evolved towards the precision and clinical excellence of aesthetic medicine. I transitioned fully from traditional beauty treatments into advanced non-surgical aesthetic treatments, focusing on achieving natural-looking, balanced results. This marked the beginning of Best Aesthetics as it is known today.

With over 10 years of experience in aesthetics, I further strengthened my clinical expertise by completing four years of full-time study at Oxford Brookes University, qualifying as a registered nurse. This allows me to combine medical knowledge with advanced aesthetic skills, ensuring the highest standards of patient safety, ethical practice, and evidence-based treatments.

Alongside running Best Aesthetics, I also work as a part-time Practice Nurse at Wallingford Medical Practice, where I have developed a strong reputation as a compassionate and attentive listener, always prioritising patient wellbeing and individual needs.

I am known for my conservative and ethical approach to aesthetics. I prioritise natural-looking results over aesthetics for the sake of aesthetics, focusing on facial harmony and long-term outcomes. I am also confident in advising clients against treatments if I believe they may not achieve excellent results or could be detrimental in the long term.

At Best Aesthetics, every treatment is tailored, carefully planned, and delivered with precision, honesty, and care. I continue to invest in ongoing training and advanced techniques to ensure my clients receive the most up-to-date and effective treatments available in modern aesthetic practice.

What I offer

Evidence-based options, explained clearly. I’ll help you choose what’s right for you — and what can wait. View the full treatment menu & prices

  • Anti-wrinkle treatments

    Prescription muscle-relaxing injections for lines and wrinkles — assessed and delivered with nursing-led care and natural movement in mind.

  • Dermal fillers

    Volume and contouring where you need it, with a focus on balance, proportion, and subtlety.

  • Lip augmentation

    Shape, definition, and hydration tailored to your features — never a one-size look.

  • Microneedling

    Collagen-stimulating skin needling to improve texture, fine lines, and overall skin quality.

  • Vitamin B12

    Intramuscular B12 for clients who are low or deficient. Vitamin B12 contributes to the reduction of tiredness and fatigue, normal energy-yielding metabolism, normal psychological function, normal functioning of the nervous system, and normal red blood cell formation. I’ll take a history and advise whether injections are appropriate for you.

  • IV Drip Therapy

    Intravenous hydration and vitamin protocols in clinic, planned after your consultation and health history — so support for energy, recovery, and wellbeing is matched to you, safely and professionally.
